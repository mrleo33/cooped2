How We Work

The company applies cutting-edge techniques to earn profits in the cryptocurrency market.

Intraday trading

Short-term trading within one trading day, aiming to profit from small price fluctuations.

Long-term trading

Opening long-term trading positions with a perspective of several months, aiming to benefit from long-term trends in the cryptocurrency market.

Swing trading

Trading with medium-term periods, ranging from a few days to a few weeks. Buying at low prices and selling at high prices based on market analysis.

Liquidity Farming

Placing cryptocurrency in a liquidity pool and receiving remuneration for providing funds to the pool.

Futures and Options Trading

Speculating on cryptocurrency derivatives to profit from both price increases and decreases in cryptocurrency

Conversion

The company provides cryptocurrency conversion services on mutually beneficial terms, providing opportunities for profit earning.
