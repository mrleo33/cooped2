Affiliate Program

Join our exclusive referral program and start earning extra income just for referring new investors!

The company's referral program provides its partners with an opportunity to rapidly multiply their income and build a partner network. The company's work is based on the linear construction of the partner network. When reaching the goal in terms of structural turnover of a certain status, you gain a new status, which provides you with more lucrative remuneration conditions, as well as opening new lines. In total, the company provides 13 statuses, as well as up to 20 lines when you reach the maximum status. When attaining certain levels you will have an opportunity to receive lucrative bonuses.
